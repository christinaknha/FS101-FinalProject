Cats and Fostering

My biggest out of work passion is working with cats. I adopted my cat Marbles during a very difficult time in my life. I was burned out from teaching and she helped me survive while I debated back and forth wether or not to leave the profession and when. About a year ago, I started fostering for CMPD Animal Care and Control. I have cared for about 20 felines ranging from newborn to 12 years old. My favorite experience with this was actually being able to witness and help a first time mama cat give birth.
